Marzipan Recipe

A Homemade Treat

Marzipan is a tasty almond-based treat that can be sculpted into edible decorations, used in baking or eaten on its own.

This recipe only uses five ingredients and, with a food processor, is a breeze to make.

Ingredients

Yields ~14oz.

  • 1-1/2c very finely ground and blanched almound flour
  • 1-1/2c powdered/confectioners sugar
  • 2tsp almond extract
  • 1tsp rose water
  • 1 large egg white

Steps

  1. Place almond flour and powdered sugar into food processor and pulse until combined.
  2. Add almond extract and rose water. Pulse until combined.
  3. Add egg white and process until thick dough is formed.
  4. Empty marzipan onto your work surface and knead a few times.
  5. Wrap it and refrigerate. Will keep for at least a month.
  6. Bring marzipan to room temperature before using it in your recipe.
